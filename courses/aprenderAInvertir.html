Learn to invest course

Who is this course for?

This course is aimed at any person interested in understanding the functioning of the markets and financial instruments and get trained to be able to manage their money independently, using the platforms of the main brokers.

At the end of this course you will be able to:

  • Understand capital market operation
  • Use indicators to interpret the economic environment
  • Analyze bonds, stocks, ETFs, etc.
  • Use investment platforms
  • Place market orders
  • Evaluate potential profitability and associated risk
  • Build an initial investment portfolio

Modality:

  • 100% live online
  • Digital course certificate

Duration:

  • 20 classes of 2 hours each
  • Ask for available days and times contact us

Included resources:

  • Downloadable study material
  • Downloadable practice material

Study Program:

  • Module 1: Introduction to financial markets
  • Module 2: Economic indicators
  • Module 3: Bonds
  • Module 4: Stocks
  • Module 5: EFTs
  • Module 6: Portfolio management
  • Module 7: Finance psychology
