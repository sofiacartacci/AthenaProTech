3. Cookies

In www.athenaprotech.com we use cookies.

A cookie consists of information sent by a web server to a web browser and saved by the latest.

We will be able to use, at any time and without advanced notice, e-commerce cookies: “PHPSESSID” and “Session”, for the purposes detailed below.

The cookie “PHPSESSID” will be used so that the website can recognize the same user going from one page to another. Without it, each page would be treated as the first visit to the website, deleting any information related to you during the navigation through our website. These cookies expire at the end of your session on our website. The cookie “session” will be destined to the user’s convenience. Our website will create these cookies with a copy of the login ID so that the user can be classified as “returning” and the content of your purchase kart remains when the user goes back to the website. If the user logs out, the login information is deleted from the cookie. This cookie will last one (1) year tops.

Besides, we use Google Analytics to analyze the usage of this website. Google Analytics generates statistical information and other types of information through the usage of cookies, that it is saved on the user’s computer. The generated information related to our website is used to create reports for the usage of the website. Google will save this information. The Privacy Policy of Google will be available at www.google.com/privacypolicy.html.

Take into account that the majority of browsers allow you to deny accepting the cookies. However, this will have a negative impact on the usability of many websites, including this one.

We can put third party ads that use cookies throughout the ads that are shown on our website and check how many times you have seen any particular ad. We do not provide any type of personal information you gave us to the usage of the cookie, so they can not personally identify you with that information on our website.
