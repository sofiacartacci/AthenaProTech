About Athena

We are an edtech organization. At Athena we seek for personal and professional growth of our students through the development of digital skills. With this aim, we offer 100% online training with special focus on practice. Our courses allow you to get trained on the most demanded digital skills. We also offer distance learning programs to prepare the talent that companies need for their digital transformation.

Mission

To transform people’s lives through quality technological training, adapted to present and future needs.

Vision

To be an educational organization that, by the use of digital skills, generates an impact in society.

Values

Professional ethics, team spirit, analytical thinking, innovation and excellence.
